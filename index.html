Key Features

Designed for both donors and food receiver

Easy Registration

Simple signup process for hotels, restaurants, and event organizers to join the platform.

Food Posting

Post surplus food with details like type, quantity, and pickup deadline.

Recipient Matching

Automatically connects you with nearby NGOs ready to collect the food.

Direct Communication

Message or call recipients directly through the platform for coordination.

Donation History

Track all your past donations and view acknowledgments from recipients.

Feedback System

Impact Reports

Track and report the impact of your food distribution efforts.

How Donesta Works

Simple steps to connect surplus food with those in need

1

Donor Posts Food

Hotels, restaurants, or event organizers register and post details about surplus food including type, quantity, and pickup time.

2

Receiver Get Notified

Nearby receiver receive instant notifications about the available donation and can view all details.

3

Connection & Pickup

The receiver accepts the donation, coordinates with the donor, and arranges for timely pickup.

4

Feedback & Tracking

The receiver gives feedback on the donation, and both parties can track the impact through the platform.

Our Impact

Making a difference in communities across India

Environmental Benefits

By redirecting surplus food to those in need, we're significantly reducing food waste that would otherwise end up in landfills. This helps reduce methane emissions and conserves the resources used to produce that food.

Community Support

Our platform enables businesses to support their local communities by providing nutritious meals to vulnerable populations. This creates stronger community bonds and helps address food insecurity in urban areas.
